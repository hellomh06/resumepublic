Foldable & Adaptive Breakwater Design

Designed a foldable breakwater system for the International Robotics Olympiad Creative category, addressing three critical coastal infrastructure challenges: cost reduction through deployable design that eliminates need for permanent concrete structures, environmental protection by preserving natural coastlines and marine ecosystems, and aesthetic preservation by remaining folded at beach edges when not in use. The system deploys automatically when sensors detect tsunami-level waves, extending across the shoreline using a folding mechanism designed to withstand water pressure while reducing wave intensity. This approach offers easy maintenance and minimal visual impact compared to traditional breakwaters, making coastal protection more accessible and sustainable.
